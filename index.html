First Opened

COSAT first opened its doors in 1999 to a very small group of talented Grade 10’s with an interest in Maths and Science, who had been invited from local schools to complete their secondary education at COSAT

2011-Current

In 2012 COSAT was the first township school to make the provinces’ top ten list for the 2011 matric exams.[6] Since its inception in 1999, the school has had a 100% matric pass rate every year except 2009. The school was placed in the ninth position, amongst the most elite schools in the Western Cape. The school, which follows the national curriculum, teaches students from grades 8 to 12. Students enter the school at grades 8 and 10. The curriculum for grades 10 to 12 includes: isiXhosa Home Language, English First Additional Language, Mathematics, Physical Science, Life Science, Information Technology ( Delphi Programming and Java) and Life Orientation. An optional class is IEB Advanced Program Mathematics.
